About My Holiday

My holiday is Christmas! This holiday is celebrated on December 25th, but most people celebrate Christmas the whole month of December. The meaning of christmas is to celebrate the birth of Jesus. The gift giving on Christmas comes from three men that brought Jesus gold, frankincense, and myrrh as gifts.

 

My special holiday means a lot to me, It brings my family together each year to my grandmas house on christmas eve. My fondest memories of Christmas was coming down to the tree and waiting for my parents to finish their cup of coffee to open presents. We also do something active on Christmas after we opened our gifts and eat a delicious breakfast.
